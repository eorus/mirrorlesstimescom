Sony officially announces the **Sony a7RIII** full frame E-mount camera.

This is a high-res full frame camera capable of 10 fps shooting with AF. Sony A7RIII features **42mp BSI CMOS sensor**, **10fps continuous shooting** with AF.  Updated design includes dual SD card slots, and **5-axis sensor based image stabilisation with up to 5.5 stops**.

The new camera also gets the 3.69M-dot OLED viewfinder from the a9, providing a 1280 x 720 resolution view. Sony claims 15 stops of dynamic range and 14-bit Raw capture across more of its shooting modes, though it still drops to 12-bit when shooting 10 fps uncompressed Raw.

**4K video recording** now supports HLG (Hybrid Log-Gamma) for **HDR video recording**, and instant playback with compatible 4K HDR TVs.

The **Sony a7R III** (model ILCE-7RM3) will start shipping from November at a recommended selling price of <a href="http://amzn.to/2h7WjaR" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>$3199</strong></a>.<!--more-->

### Sony a7RIII specifications

  * 42.4 MP with 10 FPS Continuous Shooting with either Mechanical or Electronic Shutter
  * Completely new Low vibration shutter
  * Uses the new NP-FZ100 Lithium-Ion Battery (from Sony a9)
  * USB 3.1 Type-C Tethering Connection
  * Dual SD Card Slots
  * PC Terminal
  * Pixel Shift Multi Shooting combines four sequential captures to eliminate color interpolation
  * New LSI and Image Processor for better image quality from the same 42.4 mp sensor used in a7RII
  * 4K HDR with Hybrid-Log-gamma S-Log3
  * Enhanced Eye AF
  * 5.5 Step 5-Axis Image Stabilization
  * 15 Stop Dynamic Range
  * Compatible with Sony VG-C3EM Vertical Grip (from Sony a9)
